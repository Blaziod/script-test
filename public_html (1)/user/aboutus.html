Prepared for the Air

Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the traveller - AirsExpressWay is a pioneer in worldwide online travel industry. Founded in the year 2000 by Deep Kalra, AirsExpressWay came to life to empower the Wor;d traveller with instant bookings and comprehensive choices. The company initiated its journey serving the US-Global travel market offering a range of best-value products and services powered by technology and round-the-clock customer support.

After consolidating its position in the market as a brand recognised for its reliability and transparency, AirsExpressWay launched its worldwide operations in 2005. With more and more worldwide initiating to transact online with IRCTC and new opportunities with the advent of low cost carriers, AirsExpressWay offered travellers the convenience of booking travel online with a few clicks.

AirsExpressWay’s rise has been led by the vision and the spirit of each one of its employees, for whom no idea was too big and no problem too difficult. With untiring determination, AirsExpressWay has proactively diversified its product offering, adding a variety of online and offline products and services. AirsExpressWay has stayed ahead of the curve by continually evolving its technology to meet the ever-changing demands of the rapidly developing global travel market, steadily establishing itself as worldwide’s leading online travel company.
